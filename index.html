About June on the Moon

A folk-poetic voice blending ethereal melodies with grounded storytelling. She is a Nairobi based singer, multi-instrumentalist, and producer in the Indie & Jazz scene. Her voice has made it’s way into the sphere of altrernative, soulful, & healing music. June on the Moon is a Kenyan & Caribbean singer, multi-instrumentalist, producer, and live sound engineer whose work sits at the intersection of folk, jazz, and healing music. Her voice is introspective, grounded, and ancestrally rooted, moves between acoustic intimacy and electronic architecture; speaking as much to the spirit as to the ear. In 2023 she founded Sessions on the Moon, an intimate live music platform fostering cultural exchange through acoustic and electronic performance in East Africa. She has served as Sound Designer/Producer and Technical Manager for Beneath the Baobabs, Studio Artist and Producer at Baobab Studios, and A&R at ADA Records all based in Kenya. She is currently exploring music composition for film and the curatorial design of intentional healing spaces; bringing her ceremonial practice and sonic architecture into new contexts where sound becomes environment and its medicine.

About Brown Lotus Records

Brown Lotus Records is dedicated to folk innovation and spiritual expression. Brown Lotus amplifies contemporary voices that expand traditional folk, jazz and RnB sounds into new realms — dream-folk, experimental, and cross-cultural collaborations.

Sessions on the Moon is a Folk and Blues club that curates acoustic music experiences for wanderers who enjoy soul music.

The label’s vision is to create a sacred space for artists who blur boundaries between roots and imagination, carrying folk into the future while holding space for its deep histories.
